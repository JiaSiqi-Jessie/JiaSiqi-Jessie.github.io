About

I am a Research Assistant Professor at The University of Hong Kong, specializing in urban environmental sustainability and climate adaptation. My research focuses on understanding and mitigating urban heat islands through innovative approaches in urban planning and green infrastructure design.

My work integrates geospatial analysis, environmental modeling, and urban design to address critical challenges in urban sustainability. I am particularly interested in how nature-based solutions can enhance thermal comfort and energy efficiency in dense urban environments.

Research Interests

  • Urban Heat Island & Microclimate
  • Green Infrastructure & Urban Greening
  • Urban Environmental Modeling
  • Sustainable Urban Planning
  • Building Energy Efficiency
