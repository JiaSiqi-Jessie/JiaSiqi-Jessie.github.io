Teaching Philosophy

My teaching philosophy centers on creating an engaging, inclusive, and collaborative learning environment where students can explore complex urban sustainability challenges through both theoretical understanding and hands-on experience.

Critical Thinking

Encouraging students to question assumptions, analyze complex problems, and develop evidence-based solutions for urban sustainability challenges.

Practical Application

Bridging the gap between theory and practice through real-world case studies, field work, and collaborative projects with industry partners.

Global Perspective

Fostering understanding of urban challenges as global issues requiring interdisciplinary and culturally-sensitive approaches.

Innovation & Creativity

Inspiring students to think creatively and develop innovative solutions using cutting-edge technologies and methodologies.
