Teaching Philosophy

I believe in fostering critical thinking and practical problem-solving skills in sustainable urban development. My teaching approach integrates theoretical knowledge with real-world applications, encouraging students to develop innovative solutions for urban environmental challenges.

Institution: The University of Hong Kong

Level: Graduate
