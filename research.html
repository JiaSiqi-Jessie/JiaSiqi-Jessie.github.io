Research Overview

My research focuses on urban environmental sustainability and climate adaptation, with particular emphasis on understanding and mitigating urban heat islands through innovative approaches in urban planning and green infrastructure design.

Research Areas

Urban Heat Island & Microclimate

Investigating the formation mechanisms and mitigation strategies of urban heat islands. My work combines field measurements, remote sensing, and numerical modeling to understand how urban design affects local climate conditions and thermal comfort.

Green Infrastructure & Urban Greening

Examining the cooling effects and energy-saving potentials of various green infrastructure elements, including green roofs, cool roofs, and urban forests. My research quantifies the benefits of nature-based solutions in dense urban environments.

Urban Environmental Modeling

Developing and applying advanced modeling techniques to assess urban environmental conditions. This includes machine learning approaches, geographically weighted regression, and hybrid frameworks for large-scale environmental assessment.

Building Energy Efficiency

Analyzing how urban environmental factors affect building energy consumption. My work evaluates the energy-saving potentials of various mitigation strategies under current and future climate scenarios.

Current Projects

HKU SEED Fund: AI-Based Thermal Environment Assessment

Duration: 2025 - Present

Funding: HKU SEED Fund for Basic Research

Developing advanced AI-based frameworks for thermal environment assessment and prediction, with enhanced mitigation strategies for urban thermal comfort improvement.

Global Assessment of Green Infrastructure Cooling Potential

Duration: 2023 - 2025

Comprehensive investigation of pedestrian-level cooling and energy-saving potentials of green and cool roofs across 43 global megacities, providing insights for climate-adaptive urban planning.

Research Methods

  • Remote sensing and GIS analysis
  • Urban climate modeling and simulation
  • Machine learning and deep neural networks
  • Field measurements and data collection
  • Statistical analysis and geographically weighted regression
  • Building energy simulation
